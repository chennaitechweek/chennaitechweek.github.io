Short description

Chennai Tech Week is a citywide, community-led platform for the people building what comes next across Chennai.

Boilerplate

Chennai Tech Week is a decentralized builder week across Chennai. Instead of a single venue or single-track conference, it brings together independently hosted meetups, workshops, demos, founder rooms, office hours, and community gatherings through one curated citywide calendar. The week is built for founders, engineers, designers, investors, operators, researchers, students, and communities building from Chennai.

Naming note

Use Chennai Tech Week on first mention. CTW may be used as the short form after the name has been introduced.

Logo usage

Use the supplied CTW mark or Chennai Tech Week wordmarks for press notes, community listings, decks, and event pages.

Choose the right lockup

Use CTW for tight spaces, the horizontal wordmark for headers, and the stacked wordmark for larger editorial layouts.

Use the dark-background files on dark surfaces

White and white-color files are prepared for dark backgrounds and photo overlays.

Leave clearspace

Give each logo enough room to breathe, especially around the italic Tech Week wordmark.

Do not stretch or recolor

Scale proportionally and use the supplied artwork instead of changing the palette.
